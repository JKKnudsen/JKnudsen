Glazing over ceramic!

The story of ceramics goes back at least 26k years. But whoes to say when

someone first noticed that fire can sometimes turn earth hard.

The next leap was recognizing that the clay retained whatever shape it had before

it was fired. But the nomadic lifestyle of the pre-neolithic humans

never pushed the technology further than what they could carry.

So the early farmers of the neolithic, had to make their pots from stone.

As you might have guessed this was quite labor intensive. And so it's here we see

that Ceramics evolve from simple figurines and cups. To large vessles

for long term food storage, cooking, and the seeds of glass and metallurgy.
