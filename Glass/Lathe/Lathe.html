Lathe

A lathe is crucial for precision in your lampworking. Certain tube glass to metal seals

feels close to impossible without. But it's a niche tool, and thus pricey.

But many years ago I made a prototype glass lathe, mostly out of 3D printed part.

The concept was sound, there isn't really any axial load, and the RPM's are not that high

at leat compared to a wood or metal lathe.

The prototype proved the concept. But there was room for improvments,

So a complete redesign was done in Blender
